My Story

The First Chapter

Freshman year at BYU was everything I didn't expect and everything I needed. Moving to Provo, meeting people from everywhere, and diving headfirst into college life — it was a season of firsts I'll never forget.

Between studying for physics, writing essays, exploring entrepreneurship, and finding my place on campus, I learned more about myself in one year than I had in a long time.

BYU football games, ski trips, late nights with new friends — all of it made freshman year something I'll carry with me to Samoa and beyond.

A Little About Me

Beyond the Classroom

Outside of school, one of my biggest passions is Harry Potter. I've read all seven books multiple times, and the world Rowling built has always stuck with me — the themes of loyalty, courage, and doing what's right even when it's hard.

My favorite character is Sirius Black. Wrongly imprisoned, never lost his spirit, fiercely loyal to the people he loved. There's something about that story that hits different.

I even built an interactive Marauder's Map as the web app for this project — a fully drawn Hogwarts castle with walking footprints for real characters, and a 15-question hard Harry Potter quiz layered on top. If you know your HP lore, give it a shot.
